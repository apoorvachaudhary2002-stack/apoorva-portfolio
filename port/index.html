About me

Curious designer,
empathetic listener

I'm Apoorva Chaudhary, a self-taught UX designer based in India. I started this journey because I believe technology should feel human. My process always begins with people — understanding their frustrations, motivations, and goals before I ever open Figma.

When I'm not designing, I'm sketching ideas in my journal, exploring new apps to critique, or reading about cognitive psychology.

How I work

My design process

01
Empathize

Talk to real users. Understand their world, frustrations, and what success looks like to them.

02
Define

Synthesize insights into a clear problem statement. Who are we designing for and what do they need?

03
Design

Sketch, wireframe, and prototype. Explore many ideas before committing to the right one.

04
Test

Put the design in front of real users. Learn fast, iterate faster. Repeat until it's right.
